## What Is Nano Powder?

<ImagePlaceholder />

        
Nano powder is cannabis (THC or CBD) that's been broken down into  [nanoparticles measuring 10-100 nanometers in size](https://www.rootsciences.com/blog/nano-thc-how-does-it-works/)‚Äîthat's billionths of a meter. To put it in perspective, a human hair is about 80,000 nanometers wide. We're talking impossibly tiny.
This transformation happens through[nanoemulsion technology](https://www.leafly.com/news/strains-products/nanotechnology-from-axiomm), which breaks cannabis oil into microscopic droplets and suspends them in water. The result is water-soluble cannabis that dissolves instantly in hot or cold liquids, something regular cannabis oil could never do.
Unlike the THC powder we covered in our previous guide (which uses maltodextrin and takes 15-30 minutes to kick in), nano powder uses advanced nanoemulsion technology for even faster onset‚Äîtypically 10-15 minute[s](https://www.sunmedgrowers.com/education-resources/blog/post/nano-emulsion-2024/). It's also flavorless and odorless, so it won't affect the taste of whatever you're mixing it into.
The real game-changer? Bioavailability up to 90% compared to 6-20% for traditional edibles. That means your body absorbs way more of the THC, making nano powder significantly more potent dose-for-dose. [Leafly (science section)](https://www.leafly.com/news/science-tech).

## How Nano Powder Works: The Science of Nanoemulsion

<ImagePlaceholder />

        
                Here's the problem nano powder solves: cannabinoids like THC and CBD are hydrophobic, meaning they're oil-based and don't mix with water. This is why traditional edibles have such low bioavailability (only 6-20%)‚Äîyour body struggles to absorb them efficiently.             [CDC (cannabis and health)](https://www.cdc.gov/cannabis/index.html).
            

### Here's how nanoemulsion technology works

        
				

### Extraction THC or CBD is extracted from the cannabis plant

				

### Emulsification The cannabis oil is mixed with emulsifiers (like lecithin) using high-pressure homogenization or ultrasonic waves

				

### Nano-sizing The oil droplets are broken down to 10-100 nanometers

				

### Encapsulation Tiny particles are wrapped in a protective layer of surfactants

				

### Spray drying The nanoemulsion is converted to powder form

            

### What this means for you

        
                The increased surface area allows for dramatically faster absorption. Instead of passing through your entire digestive system and liver, nano particles absorb directly through the mucous membranes in your mouth and stomach. This means more THC enters your bloodstream, delivering higher potency with a lower dose.**Think of it like this: regular edibles are like trying to dissolve a rock in water, while nano powder is like dissolving sugar. One works instantly; the other takes forever and barely dissolves at all. ## Why Nano Powder Is Faster & Stronger Than Regular Edibles Speed comparison**             [NIH (PubMed)](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/).
